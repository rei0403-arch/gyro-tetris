Gyro Tetris

A motion-control Android Tetris prototype. Tilt to move, spin the phone to rotate, preview the landing spot, hard-drop with volume keys, and compare scores through Firebase leaderboard support.

Download APK
Current build
Firebase anonymous login, global ranking, combo scoring, smooth rotation, landing ghost, preview settings, pause/retry, and hold-to-lock input.

Controls

MoveTilt the phone left or right. Stronger tilt moves faster.
RotateSpin the phone smoothly. The locked piece snaps to the nearest right angle.
Hard dropPress volume up and volume down together.
SettingsUse SET to tune movement and rotation sensitivity with a live preview.
Lock inputHold the screen to freeze tilt and rotation input while the piece keeps falling.
LeaderboardUse RANK in the app to view Firebase global scores.

Leaderboard
